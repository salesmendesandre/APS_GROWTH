# Call for Proposals and Funding

The **APS-GROWTH** project is part of the Service-Learning project calls at the University of Salamanca (USAL), supported by the **Social Affairs Service of the University of Salamanca (SAS)**.

Throughout its different editions, the project has received the necessary institutional and financial support to acquire hardware, develop infrastructure, and facilitate the implementation of the system in the real working ecosystem, which includes not only the **Lorenzo Milani Center** but also the **inclusive garden of the SAS** (*huertita inclusiva del SAS*).

Below is a breakdown of the funding obtained in each of the project's calls:

## APS-GROWTH I

**Call 2024-2025**

In its first edition, the project laid the technological and accessibility foundations. Purchases included **BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy) beacons**, waterproof enclosures, laminated QR codes, and physical supports to create interactive information points in the garden. In addition, a **weather station** was acquired to begin basic environmental monitoring of the surroundings.

- **Funding obtained:** 860 €

## APS-GROWTH II

**Call 2025-2026**

During this period, the project made the leap towards a remote IoT sensor network. The budget was allocated to the purchase of **LoRa communication nodes and a Gateway** (long-range and low-power) with an external antenna, as well as **solar panels and batteries** to provide the devices with energy autonomy. A full deployment of agricultural sensors (capacitive soil moisture, ambient temperature and humidity, and light) was also acquired, along with waterproof enclosures (IP65) and electronic components for their integration.

- **Funding obtained:** 1,320 €

## APS-GROWTH III

**Call 2026-2027**

In the current period, the focus is on physical action upon the environment through the **complete automation of the irrigation system**. Purchases include 12V solenoid valves, flow meters, control relays, and LoRa microcontrollers. Furthermore, all necessary hydraulic infrastructure is funded (pipes, branches, shut-off valves), along with protection systems, electrical wiring, and final installation to operate outdoors safely and autonomously.

- **Funding obtained:** 1,300 €
