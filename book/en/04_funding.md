# Call for Proposals and Funding

The **APS-GROWTH** project is part of the Service-Learning project calls at the University of Salamanca (USAL). Throughout its different editions, it has received the necessary institutional and financial support to acquire hardware, develop infrastructure, and facilitate the implementation of the system in the real environment of the Lorenzo Milani Center's garden.

Below is a breakdown of the funding obtained in each year of the project:

## APS-GROWTH I (First year)

In its first edition, the project laid the technological and methodological foundations, acquiring the first sensors, microcontrollers, and basic tools for initial prototyping.

- **Funding obtained:** 860 €

## APS-GROWTH II (Second year)

During the second year, the project expanded to include long-range communication modules (LoRa), solar power systems, batteries, and waterproof nodes for outdoor deployment.

- **Funding obtained:** 1,320 €

## APS-GROWTH III (Third year)

In the third year, the focus is on complete automation, including solenoid valves, flow meters, hydraulic infrastructure, and protection systems and final electrical installation.

- **Funding obtained:** 1,300 €
