# Call for Proposals and Funding

The **APS-GROWTH** project is part of the Service-Learning project calls at the University of Salamanca (USAL), supported by the **Social Affairs Service of the University of Salamanca (SAS)**.

Throughout its different editions, the project has received the necessary institutional and financial support to acquire hardware, develop infrastructure, and facilitate the implementation of the system in the real working ecosystem, which includes not only the **Lorenzo Milani Center** but also the **inclusive garden of the SAS** (*huertita inclusiva del SAS*).

Below is a breakdown of the funding obtained in each of the project's calls:

## APS-GROWTH I (Call 2024-2025)

In its first edition, the project laid the technological and methodological foundations, acquiring the first sensors, microcontrollers, and basic tools for initial prototyping.

- **Funding obtained:** 860 €

## APS-GROWTH II (Call 2025-2026)

During this period, the project expanded to include long-range communication modules (LoRa), solar power systems, batteries, and waterproof nodes for outdoor deployment.

- **Funding obtained:** 1,320 €

## APS-GROWTH III (Call 2026-2027)

In the current period, the focus is on complete automation, including solenoid valves, flow meters, hydraulic infrastructure, and protection systems and final electrical installation.

- **Funding obtained:** 1,300 €
