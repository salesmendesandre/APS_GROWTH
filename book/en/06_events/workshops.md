# Technology Workshops

One of the most prominent activities is the technology training workshops, aimed at teaching the fundamentals of the Internet of Things (IoT) in a practical and engaging way.

## Workshop: LoRaWAN and Assembly of an Environmental IoT Node

As part of the **APS-GROWTH II Service-Learning Project**, a practical workshop was organized focusing on the assembly and understanding of a sensor node based on LPWAN technologies.

### Program and Contents

The workshop consisted of the following thematic blocks, combining theory with extensive hands-on practice:

1. **Introduction to LoRa and LoRaWAN**: Technological foundations for long-distance communication in the Internet of Things.
2. **Node components**: Identification of the key elements of the architecture.
3. **Assembly and sensors**: Physical assembly of the node and connection of the environmental sensors.
4. **Data transmission and monitoring**: Configuring the network for use in an educational and inclusive garden.

Under the motto *"Technology, sustainability, and inclusion"*, the workshop successfully trained attendees in cutting-edge technologies applied to real-world, sustainable environments.
